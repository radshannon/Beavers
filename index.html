About Us

Our Humble Beginnings

12th January 2018: the night was cold and the beer was flowing. On this special night (with no influence of alcohol at all) three queen beavers conceived an idea that would change the course of history forever. An idea so great, so brilliant that it birthed the greatest group that ever was.

“Hey, let’s start our own softball team!”

Several more drinks and some (partially) sober planning later, and the GLSML Beavers were born.

The queens were then tasked with the job of recruiting their clunge* of beavers. They sought out to build a super team, a team so talented, so unbeatable that they would be the ultimate force to be reckoned with... at the pub.

The 2018 Beavers proved their infinite talents. Balls were hit. Games were played. But most importantly fun was had and the team proved themselves to be the most fun and friendly team in the league.

*clunge: a badass group of beavers (I encourage you to Google this)
